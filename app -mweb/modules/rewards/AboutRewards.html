About the program

It pays to be a Staples Rewards® member. When you sign up, you'll earn up to 5% back in rewards on your purchases — from your office's favorite supplies and snacks to technology and more. Plus get free shipping on orders over $14.99 on staples.com (Oversize items excluded). And with more than 1,000,000 products to choose from, you'll find everything your business needs in one easy place.

Up to 5% back in rewards

From technology and business services to cleaning supplies and coffee, earn up to 5% back in rewards on everything in store and online. Base members earn 2% back in rewards, Plus members earn 3% back in rewards, and Premier members earn 5% back in rewards. Not valid on postage, gift/phone cards and savings passes.There's no limit to the rewards you can earn.

Ink recycling rewards

Earn $2 back in rewards for every ink or toner cartridge you recycle. Minimum purchase requirements must be met.

Plus and Premier Teacher members

Enjoy additional benefits and savings when you achieve Plus or Premier status.

Plus members earn 3% back in rewards, and Premier members earn 5% back in rewards. Plus and Premier membership is based on annual spend. Plus members spend $500-$999 annually, and Premier members spend $1,000 or more annually.

Free EasyTech Service and Savings

Plus and Premier members receive one free diagnostic service for hardware or software per year. Premier members also get $20 towards Total Support Protection Plan.

Free Copy & Print Savings

(Premier members only)

Plus members get $25 of free Copy & Print services and Premier members get $50 of free Copy & Print services.
